Elephant in the Room is designed by students for students. We recognized a lack of willingness to discuss mental health issues commonly faced by students, leaving many feeling lonely and isolated. Our aim was to create a safe space for students to ask questions that they are often embarrassed to ask friends or family. We hope this site encourages people to ask questions regarding the many stresses facing students, and most importantly helps them realize that they are far from alone.
